Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Zuultimate for free?

Yes. The Community tier is free forever under the AGPL-3.0 license. It includes JWT auth, basic vault, and the middleware stack.

What happens if I exceed my usage limits?

Overage is billed at the rates shown above. You will receive a warning at 80% of your quota. Service is never interrupted.

Can I switch tiers at any time?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes. Annual billing saves approximately 17% compared to monthly billing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice.

Is there a self-hosted option?

Zuultimate is open-source under AGPL-3.0. You can self-host with Community features. Pro and Enterprise features require a valid license key.
